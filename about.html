TRENT POPE

"The urge to destroy is also a creative urge."

- MIKHAIL BAKUNIN

Trent Pope is an aspiring digital and material artist from Utah County. He had never claimed artistry until he realized that it was the closest title he could put to what he was up to.

Some of his notable works include the work he's done on the DREAMWALK PARK project, the rework series, STRIPEDSLEEPWATERGLASS, his 2019 poem To Seattle, Sincerely Salt Lake, and other small experiments.

Most of his work remains unpublished and is only accessible through word of mouth or a direct relation.
